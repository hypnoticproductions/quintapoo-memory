## CTA PARTNERSHIP

### Community Tourism Agency (CTA)
- **CEO**: Dahlia Guard (15+ years experience in event management, business development, strategic project management)
- **Launch**: Anse-la-Raye, 2023 (3 years ago)
- **Mission**: Empower micro, small, and medium-size enterprises (MSMEs) in tourism sector
- **Goal**: Ensure Saint Lucians have voice in destination management, economic benefits of tourism reach communities

### Funding Structure
- **Model**: Loan/grant arrangement

- **Lending Packages**: Up to XCD$300,000

- **2025 Investment**: Over EC$900,000 through Tourism Enhancement Fund
- **CARICOM Funding**: USD $2,343,907.60 allocated for community-driven tourism projects
- **Regional Expansion**: CTA model praised by CARICOM Development Fund, looking to implement across Caribbean
- **Coverage**: Working with communities from Gros Islet to Vieux Fort (north, south, east, west)
